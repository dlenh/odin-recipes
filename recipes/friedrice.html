Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi, or fermented cabbage, is a staple in Korean cuisine. Each family has their own kimchi recipe passed on through generations. In addition to being a side dish, it can be incorporated into dishes like kimchi stew and kimchi fried rice.

Fried rice is a canvas in the sense that a plethora of ingredients can be added to it. Kimchi is one of those ingredients and it adds a beautiful red-orange color, and a distinct tangy, umami taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice, cooked
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang
  • 1 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Stir fry kimchi in a pan with the vegetable oil.
  2. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Mix ingredients until well incoporated.
  3. Add sesame oil and remove from heat.
  4. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  5. Enjoy!
